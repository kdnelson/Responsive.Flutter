

---------- Flutter Responsive Architecture ----------

Responsive Block (RB) 
	|--> DeviceTypeWidget (SL) "What type of device do we have here?"
	|--> OrientationWidget (SL) "Are we in Portrait or Landscape?"
	(For every orientation change, have LayoutBuilder rebuild the context)

main
|-->MainApplication
	|-->HomeResponsiveWidget extends BaseWidget<HomeViewModel>
		|-->RB
			|--> Call the appropriate widget of 4 ie. MobileMenuPortraitWidget (SF)
				|--> DrawerWidget AND |--> MobileHomePortraitWidget (SF) (All 4 widgets exits the responsive directory)
					|-->RB			  	|--> Text(Print vm.title)
						|--> Call the appropriate widget of 4 ie. MobileDrawerPortraitWidget (SF)
							|--> DrawerOptionsHelper (Class)
								|--> List<DrawOptionsWidget>
									|--> Each element calls Provider.of<DrawItemModel>
										|--> RB
											|--> Call the appropriate widget of 4 ie. MobileDrawerPortraitOptionsWidget (SF)
												|--> DrawItemModel.Object is rendered per RB
